# Incident Response

This section focuses on the use of AI to assist incident responders during the identification, analysis, containment, eradication, recovery, and reporting phases of security incidents.

Modern security operations generate large volumes of alerts, logs, telemetry, and forensic artifacts. AI can help analysts rapidly summarize findings, identify indicators of compromise, prioritize response actions, and communicate risk to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

## Topics Covered

* Incident Triage
* Account Compromise Investigations
* Cloud Account Compromise Investigations
* Business Email Compromise (BEC)
* Malware Triage
* Ransomware Investigations
* Indicators of Compromise (IOC) Analysis
* Identity-Based Attacks
* Containment and Recovery Planning
* Post-Incident Analysis

## Objectives

* Demonstrate AI-assisted incident response workflows
* Improve consistency in investigative analysis
* Accelerate incident triage and decision-making
* Generate structured response recommendations
* Support communication between technical teams and leadership
* Improve incident documentation and reporting

## Use Cases

### Account Compromise Investigation

Analyze authentication activity, account misuse indicators, and unauthorized access patterns to determine the likelihood and scope of compromise.

### Cloud Account Compromise Investigation

Investigate suspicious activity involving Microsoft 365, Entra ID, Azure resources, and cloud administration actions to identify unauthorized access and potential impact.

### Business Email Compromise (BEC)

Investigate executive impersonation, payment fraud attempts, and malicious email activity to assess risk and recommend response actions.

### Malware Triage Investigation

Review endpoint activity, process execution, network communications, persistence mechanisms, and security alerts to assess potential malware infections.

### Ransomware Investigation

Analyze ransomware-related activity, identify indicators of compromise, assess business impact, and recommend containment and recovery actions.

## Each Use Case Includes

* Incident scenario
* Example prompt
* Example output
* Risk assessment methodology
* Recommended response actions
* Operational value

## Why AI Helps

AI can rapidly summarize incident data, correlate indicators, identify attack patterns, and generate structured investigative outputs. This allows incident responders to spend more time validating evidence, coordinating response efforts, containing threats, and restoring business operations while maintaining consistency across investigations.
