# Insider Threat Case Summary

## Objective

Use AI to transform a detailed insider threat investigation into a concise summary suitable for Security Leadership, Human Resources, Legal Counsel, and Business Stakeholders.

## Scenario

An insider threat investigation identified unusual file access activity involving a departing employee. Investigators collected evidence from endpoint telemetry, DLP alerts, access logs, and employee interviews. Leadership requires a concise case summary.

## Prompt

You are a Senior Insider Threat Investigator.

Review the following investigative findings and generate a stakeholder-ready case summary.

Investigation Findings:

* Employee submitted resignation notice
* Accessed sensitive engineering documentation outside normal responsibilities
* Downloaded 2,300 files within 72 hours of departure
* Uploaded archive files to personal cloud storage
* DLP alerts generated for sensitive content movement
* No approved business justification identified
* Manager confirmed unusual activity patterns

Provide:

1. Executive Summary
2. Risk Assessment
3. Key Findings
4. Business Impact
5. Recommended Actions
6. Case Status

## Example Output

### Executive Summary

An investigation into a departing employee identified multiple indicators associated with potential intellectual property theft. Evidence suggests the employee accessed and transferred sensitive company information shortly before separation from the organization.

### Risk Assessment

High

### Key Findings

* Elevated file access activity
* Access outside normal job responsibilities
* Large-scale file downloads
* Transfer of data to personal cloud storage
* Multiple DLP policy violations
* Lack of documented business justification

### Business Impact

The activity created potential risk to intellectual property, confidential business information, and competitive advantage. Further review of accessed content may be necessary to determine exposure.

### Recommended Actions

* Preserve investigative evidence
* Coordinate with Legal and Human Resources
* Review content classifications
* Assess contractual and regulatory obligations
* Restrict access to sensitive resources where appropriate

### Case Status

Investigation complete. Findings referred to Legal, Human Resources, and Security Leadership for review and disposition.

## Why AI Helps

AI can rapidly summarize lengthy investigations, identify key findings, and generate consistent reports tailored for non-technical stakeholders. This helps investigative teams reduce reporting effort while improving communication and decision-making.
