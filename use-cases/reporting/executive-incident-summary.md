# Executive Incident Summary

## Objective

Use AI to transform technical incident data into concise, executive-friendly summaries that communicate risk, impact, and response actions.

## Scenario

A ransomware incident affected multiple workstations within the finance department. Technical responders collected detailed forensic evidence, but leadership requires a concise summary suitable for decision-making.

## Prompt

You are a Cybersecurity Incident Response Manager preparing a briefing for executive leadership.

Review the following incident details and create an executive summary.

Incident Details:

* Employee opened a malicious email attachment
* Malware executed via PowerShell
* 14 finance workstations encrypted
* No evidence of domain controller compromise
* Backups remained unaffected
* Incident contained within 3 hours
* Systems restored from backups
* No customer data exposure identified

Provide:

1. Executive Summary
2. Business Impact
3. Response Actions
4. Current Status
5. Recommended Leadership Actions

## Example Output

### Executive Summary

A ransomware incident impacted 14 finance department workstations after a malicious email attachment was opened. The attack was rapidly identified and contained before spreading to critical infrastructure or backup systems.

### Business Impact

Temporary disruption to finance operations occurred during containment and recovery activities. No customer data exposure or loss of critical business systems has been identified.

### Response Actions

* Isolated affected systems
* Removed malicious artifacts
* Restored systems from backups
* Conducted forensic investigation
* Enhanced monitoring for related activity

### Current Status

Recovery activities have been completed and affected systems have been restored. No evidence of ongoing malicious activity has been identified.

### Recommended Leadership Actions

* Support ongoing security awareness training
* Review ransomware response procedures
* Continue investment in backup and recovery capabilities
* Monitor post-incident remediation efforts

## Why AI Helps

AI can rapidly convert technical findings into language that executives can understand, enabling faster decision-making and more effective communication during security incidents.
