# Threat Landscape Analysis

## Objective

Use AI to analyze current threat activity, identify relevant threat actors, and summarize the evolving threat landscape for security teams and leadership.

## Scenario

Security leadership requests a summary of the current external threat landscape to understand which threat actors, attack techniques, and trends pose the greatest risk to the organization.

## Prompt

You are a Senior Threat Intelligence Analyst.

Review the following threat activity and provide a threat landscape assessment.

Observed Threat Activity:

* Increase in ransomware attacks targeting enterprises
* Growth in business email compromise campaigns
* Continued remote worker fraud operations
* Increased identity-based attacks targeting cloud environments
* Multiple reports of software supply chain compromise attempts

Provide:

1. Executive Summary
2. Significant Threat Actors
3. Primary Attack Techniques
4. Industry Impact
5. Recommended Defensive Priorities

## Example Output

### Executive Summary

The threat landscape continues to be dominated by financially motivated cybercriminal groups leveraging ransomware, business email compromise, identity attacks, and fraud operations. Organizations should prioritize identity security, phishing resistance, and incident response preparedness.

### Significant Threat Actors

* Ransomware operators
* Business email compromise groups
* Remote worker fraud networks
* Credential theft operators
* Supply chain threat actors

### Primary Attack Techniques

* Social engineering
* Credential theft
* MFA bypass
* Remote access abuse
* Malware deployment
* Data exfiltration

### Industry Impact

Organizations continue to face operational disruption, financial loss, intellectual property theft, and increased regulatory scrutiny resulting from successful cyber intrusions.

### Recommended Defensive Priorities

* Strengthen identity protections
* Improve phishing resistance
* Enhance monitoring and detection
* Review third-party risk management
* Conduct incident response exercises

## Why AI Helps

AI can rapidly consolidate threat intelligence reporting, identify common themes across multiple threat sources, and generate concise assessments that support security planning and decision-making.
