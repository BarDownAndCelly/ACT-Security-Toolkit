# Privileged Access Abuse Investigation

## Objective

Use AI to assist insider threat investigators in identifying potential abuse of privileged access, unauthorized data access, and misuse of administrative permissions.

## Scenario

A privileged employee begins accessing systems and data repositories outside their normal responsibilities. Security leadership requests an investigation to determine whether the activity represents legitimate administrative work or potential misuse of privileged access.

## Prompt

You are a Senior Insider Threat Investigator.

Review the following activity and determine whether it indicates potential abuse of privileged access.

Employee:

John Smith

Role:

Systems Administrator

Observed Activity:

* Accessed multiple sensitive repositories outside assigned responsibilities
* Downloaded administrative reports unrelated to current projects
* Queried employee records without documented business justification
* Access activity increased significantly during off-hours
* Multiple privileged actions performed without associated change requests
* Access patterns deviated from historical behavior

Provide:

1. Executive Summary
2. Risk Rating
3. Indicators of Privileged Access Abuse
4. Alternative Explanations
5. Recommended Investigative Actions
6. Potential Business Risks

## Example Output

### Executive Summary

The activity contains several indicators consistent with potential privileged access abuse. Access outside assigned responsibilities, unexplained administrative actions, and behavioral deviations warrant further investigation.

### Risk Rating

High

### Indicators of Privileged Access Abuse

* Access beyond assigned responsibilities
* Unauthorized review of sensitive records
* Significant off-hours activity
* Missing change management documentation
* Behavioral deviations from established baseline

### Alternative Explanations

* Emergency operational support
* Approved administrative projects
* Incident response activities
* Temporary role assignments

### Recommended Investigative Actions

* Review privileged access logs
* Validate change management records
* Conduct manager interviews
* Review historical administrative activity
* Assess data accessed during the review period

### Potential Business Risks

* Unauthorized disclosure of sensitive information
* Regulatory violations
* Data exfiltration
* Insider threat activity
* Abuse of trusted access

## Why AI Helps

AI can rapidly correlate privileged access activity, behavioral anomalies, and business context to generate structured investigative assessments and support insider threat investigations.
