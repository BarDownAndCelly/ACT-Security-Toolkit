# Business Email Compromise Investigation

## Objective

Use AI to assist incident responders in investigating potential business email compromise (BEC) incidents, identifying fraudulent activity, and recommending containment actions.

## Scenario

An employee reports receiving an email appearing to originate from a senior executive requesting an urgent wire transfer. Funds were transferred before the request was verified.

## Prompt

You are a Senior Incident Responder.

Review the following incident details.

Observed Activity:

* Email spoofed executive identity
* Request for urgent wire transfer
* Employee completed transfer
* Email originated from external domain
* No prior vendor relationship existed

Provide:

1. Executive Summary
2. Risk Rating
3. Indicators of Compromise
4. Business Impact
5. Recommended Response Actions

## Example Output

### Executive Summary

A business email compromise incident resulted in an unauthorized financial transaction after an employee acted on a fraudulent request impersonating executive leadership.

### Risk Rating

Critical

### Indicators of Compromise

* Executive impersonation
* External sender domain
* Financial urgency
* Unverified payment request

### Business Impact

Potential financial loss and reputational risk.

### Recommended Response Actions

* Contact financial institution immediately
* Preserve email evidence
* Review affected accounts
* Conduct user awareness follow-up
* Notify appropriate stakeholders

## Why AI Helps

AI can rapidly summarize evidence, identify fraud indicators, and assist responders in producing consistent investigative reports.
