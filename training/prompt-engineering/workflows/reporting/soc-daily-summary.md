# SOC Daily Summary

## Objective

Use AI to consolidate security alerts, investigations, and incident activity into a concise daily summary for Security Operations Center (SOC) analysts, team leads, and security management.

## Scenario

The SOC processes thousands of alerts daily. Leadership requires a concise operational summary highlighting significant activity, active investigations, and emerging risks.

## Prompt

You are a Senior SOC Analyst.

Review the following security activity and generate a daily SOC summary.

Security Activity:

* 12,847 alerts processed
* 37 alerts escalated for investigation
* 3 phishing incidents confirmed
* 1 account compromise investigation opened
* 2 malware detections contained
* 1 DLP investigation initiated
* No critical outages reported

Provide:

1. Executive Summary
2. Significant Events
3. Active Investigations
4. Emerging Risks
5. Recommended Follow-Up Actions

## Example Output

### Executive Summary

SOC operations remained stable with no critical security incidents impacting business operations. Multiple phishing and malware-related events were identified and successfully contained.

### Significant Events

* Three phishing incidents confirmed
* Two malware detections contained
* One account compromise investigation initiated
* One DLP investigation opened

### Active Investigations

* Account compromise involving suspicious MFA activity
* DLP investigation involving external data transfer

### Emerging Risks

* Increased phishing activity targeting employees
* Continued identity-focused attack attempts

### Recommended Follow-Up Actions

* Continue monitoring active investigations
* Review phishing trends
* Validate containment effectiveness
* Update detection content as needed

## Why AI Helps

AI can rapidly summarize large volumes of security activity, identify significant operational trends, and generate consistent reporting for SOC leadership while reducing analyst reporting workload.
